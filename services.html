Our Services

Tutorials

At Boma's Intellectual Academy, we provide high-quality tutorials for students preparing for exams like JAMB, WAEC, NECO, IJMB, JUPEB, and more. Our tutorials are designed to help students understand key concepts and excel in their exams.

Welfare

We care about the overall well-being of our students. Our welfare programs ensure that students receive the necessary support, motivation, and resources to succeed academically and personally.

Educational Consulting

Our educational consulting services help students and parents make informed decisions about their academic paths. We offer guidance on course selection, exam preparation, and college admissions.
